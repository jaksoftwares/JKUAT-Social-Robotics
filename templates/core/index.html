ABOUT JKUAT SOCIAL ROBOTICS

At JKUAT Social Robotics Lab, we aim to design and develop robots that learn to interact socially in human environments. The research work at the lab focuses on the following areas: Education, Social Training, Assistive Robots, and Responsible Computing. We aim to embed human-centered and responsible computing principles — Inclusivity and Accessibility, Ethical Design (fairness, transparency, trust, privacy), and Societal Impact (economic, social, empathy) — to build effective and intuitive interactions between people and robotic technologies in human environments.
